About Me

Hello, my name is Ahmed Safi Ahmed Shehata. My passion for security began in my first year at college, when I became fascinated by how networks connect the world — and how they can be protected. Over the past five years, I’ve grown into an Information Security Analyst with hands-on experience in tools like Wireshark, and a strong background in networking and security.

Today, I focus on helping organizations secure their infrastructures by identifying vulnerabilities, analyzing risks, and applying industry best practices.

My ultimate goal is to build trusted digital environments where businesses can thrive without fear of hidden threats.

Outside of work, I enjoy continuous learning, taking on challenges, and turning complex security problems into practical, effective solutions.
